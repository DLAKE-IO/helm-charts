WEBHOOK LISTENER (Kubernetes audit log collection):
  The Wazuh audit webhook listener sidecar is deployed in the manager-master pod.
  To complete setup:

  1. Configure your Kubernetes API server with:
       --audit-policy-file=/path/to/audit-policy.yaml
       --audit-webhook-config-file=/path/to/webhook-kubeconfig.yaml

  2. Your reference audit policy (if enabled) is in ConfigMap:
       {{ include "wazuh.fullname" . }}-audit-policy

  3. Create a kubeconfig pointing to the webhook listener Service:
       server: https://{{ include "wazuh.fullname" . }}-webhook-listener.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local

  4. Extract the CA cert from the TLS Secret for your webhook kubeconfig:
       kubectl get secret {{ .Values.webhookListener.tls.existingSecret | default (printf "%s-webhook-listener-tls" (include "wazuh.fullname" .)) }} \
         -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} \
         -o jsonpath='{.data.ca\.crt}' | base64 -d > /tmp/wazuh-webhook-ca.crt
     Place the base64-encoded value under clusters[0].cluster.certificate-authority-data
     in your webhook kubeconfig.
